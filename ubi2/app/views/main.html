UbiDoBi is a free project management and collaboration service
UbiDoBi isn't just about sharing to-do lists with colleagues. We look at every aspect of delivering a product and include unique, intuitive tools to help you with
  • Brainstorming
  • System design
  • Troubleshooting
  • Online marketing
Some of the many tools to make life easier include:
  • Cloud-based collaboration
  • Automated project reports
  • Real-time dashboards
  • Shared, interactive workspaces, including document reviews and whiteboards
  • Any device, anywhere
  • Push notifications

...take one bite at a time

In the words of Creighton Abrams, "When eating an elephant take one bite at a time". when faced with a daunting, complex problem, it is best to take it one step at a time.

UbiDoBi is built on this premise, where even the most challenging projects can be delegated and decomposed into a collection of manageable, coordinated to-do lists.
